Personal Epistemic Prosthesis (PEP)

Judgment Capture (Offline Artifact Generator)

This page does not provide advice, analysis, recommendations, or decisions. It exists solely to help you record your own judgment in a structured way.

All data stays in your browser. Nothing is stored, sent, or evaluated. The only output is a file you explicitly choose to download.

Context Briefly describe the situation as you understand it now. One or two short paragraphs is usually enough. Optional. Use broad labels (e.g., career, relationships, ethics). Optional. Note time pressure, obligations, or limits you can’t change.
Decision List the real options you see, even if some feel unlikely. What you are leaning toward or have decided, for now.
Rationale Explain why this option feels acceptable. A few sentences is usually sufficient. Which timeframe matters most for this judgment?
Affective Signals These numbers are expressive, not scientific. Use your own sense of proportion.
